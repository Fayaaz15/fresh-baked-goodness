✦ Our Story ✦

Born from a
Passion for Purity

Fresh Baked Goodness began with a simple, unwavering belief — that extraordinary desserts don't need eggs to be extraordinary. Rooted in Chennai's Mylapore neighbourhood, we've spent over a decade proving that egg-free baking can be just as rich, indulgent, and memorable as anything else.

Every cake we craft is a labour of love. We source only the finest ingredients, honour the trust of our customers, and refuse to compromise on taste or texture — ever.
